### Services

**Wedding Photography & Videography** — Professional Wedding Photography (2-10+ hrs), Professional Wedding Videography (2-10+ hrs), Drone, Content Creator

**Family Portraits** — 30 mins mini sessions (recommended max. 6 ppl), 1hr sessions (3 - 15+ ppl)

**Couple's Portraits** — 30 mins mini sessions, 1hr sessions, Proposals / Engagements, 2hr Deluxe Sessions, 4hr Adventure Sessions

### FAQ

**What are your prices like?**

Please inquire to receive access to prices and packages. Wedding Photography Packages starting at $2,000. Couples & Family Photoshoots starting at $700. Custom Packages & Pricing available upon request!

### How it works

**1. Inquiry email** — Once you inquire you will receive more information with packages and prices…then we will chat over emails or a zoom call, to discuss the vision and decide if I am the photographer for you!

**2. Booking process** — Booking can be done online, it's quick and easy! I take an online deposit for each and every session or wedding booked, and I work on a first-come, first-served basis. For weddings, we recommend you book at least 6 months prior to your date.

**3. Preparations / the day has come!!!** — After discussing the plan, we will meet on the day, loosen up and chat first before we start taking any photos! I will help pose you, help with any outfit choices and do whatever I can to bring our vision to reality.

**4. Receive your photos / videos!** — You will receive an online link to all your photos stored in a personal online gallery. Share them with friends, print canvases and design albums in the highest quality possible.

### Photography style

_My Photography style is a mixture of documentarian and editorial…_

Which means sometimes I'll pose you for those show-stopping photos, but most of the time I'll be capturing everything as it happens naturally, with authentic emotion. I'll always make a point of ensuring you feel as natural and relaxed as possible before I take any photos. You may even hear me cracking jokes in between shots, it's like we're just having a conversation!

_My editing is timeless, vibrant, and sometimes a dramatic black and white…_ it all depends on what your love story is, the emotions that were there in the moment, and how I want you to feel when you look back onto your beautiful lifelong memories.
