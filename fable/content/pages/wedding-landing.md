##  FAQs

  * ####  How does your booking process work?

I always recommend booking at least 6 months prior to have the best chance of guaranteeing your desired date. A deposit is required to confirm your date, this ranges from 30-50% of the total cost, and is non-refundable. The remaining balance is due 4 weeks prior to the wedding day.

  * ####  How long will it take to receive my final gallery?

You can expect your final gallery within 8 weeks from your wedding day, sometimes faster depending on my schedule / the size of your booking. I will always try my best to also throw in a sneak peek so you have something to look back on!

  * ####  Will I receive all the photos from my wedding day?

No, you will receive all the best photos. I have had friends complain to me in the past that their wedding photographer sent them way too many photos. The good, the bad and the ugly. ALL in a single link. So when she tried to spend an evening with her family watching her wedding slideshow, all they saw was 5 of the same photo taken with eyes closed, bad lighting, etc… imagine doing that for 4000 photos… you’d never look at your wedding photos again!!!

I never send RAW files. All final photos in your gallery will be professionally and meticulously edited, and delivered to you via an online link. You can expect to receive up to 80 final photos per hour of wedding photography booked. I do not cap the total number of photos for weddings as I believe after paying a luxury price you deserve a luxury experience and I don’t charge you for any extra photos. If a photo isn’t how I would like it to be, but it’s a special memory / moment from the day, I’ll make sure you have it to remember that moment forever.

  * ####  Can you work with all venues in Aruba?

Yes. Once you have chosen a venue, it’s important to keep me in the loop. Even exclusive venues such as Marriott Stellaris only allow a handful of photographers to work on premises, but I am on their vendor list too.

  * ####  What are your prices like?

Packages start at $2499. Once you inquire, you will be sent a pricing guide and a link to book a call with me. We will discuss which package may be best for you and any other services I can provide, and you’ll get a better idea about planning your wedding… I’ve helped countless couples before with freebies and wedding planning guides! You can download yours at the bottom of this page…

  * ####  Do you offer videography too?

Yes, I offer both Wedding Photography & Videography services.

  * ####  Do you travel for weddings?

Yes, I travel for work. I currently do not charge for airfare for some destinations, as I am an avid traveller and love traveling for work! However there may be a minimum spend depending on the destination. London / Aruba both have no extra fees involved.

Inquire to see if your destination includes free travel!
