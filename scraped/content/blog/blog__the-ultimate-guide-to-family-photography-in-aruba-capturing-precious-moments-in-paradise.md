## Best Locations In Aruba For Family Photoshoots

Selecting the right backdrop is key for capturing beautiful family portraits. Here are some of the best Aruba family portrait spots, the perfect backdrop for weddings and proposals as well. Some of the family's favorite spots are as follows.

  * Eagle Beach is one of the famous beaches for its soft white sand and Divi Divi trees. This location offers a classic Aruba beach setting with stunning ocean views.

  * Baby Beach has a unique, shallow turquoise lagoon perfect for families with young children, providing a calm and scenic environment.

  * Arashi Beach is popular for its crystal-clear waters and rocky coastline, adding a unique touch to family portraits.

  * California Lighthouse serves as a historical landmark with panoramic views of the island, offering a breathtaking backdrop.

  * Bushiribana Gold Mill Ruins is ideal for families looking for a mix of history and rustic charm. This location provides dramatic textures and unique scenery.

  * Palm Beach – Ideal for sunset photography, Palm Beach offers a lively yet picturesque setting with beautiful golden-hour lighting.

  * Mangel Halto is another hidden gem with mangroves and shallow waters, perfect for intimate and serene family portraits.

  * Alto Vista Chapel can be one of the peaceful and scenic spots that offers a spiritual and historical touch to your family photos.

## Tips for a Perfect Family Photography in Aruba

You do not want to ruin your precious family photoshoot over tiny factors. To avoid this, we have created a few tips that make your photoshoot foolproof. To make sure that your Aruba beach photoshoot goes smoothly, consider these essential tips.

### Choosing the Right Time

The best time to take photos in Aruba is during the golden hour, which occurs in the morning, the hour after sunrise, or the hour before sunset. The light is soft and warm, producing a beautiful glow in the photographs.

### Outfit for the Occasion

Light, neutral, or pastel colors work well on the beach, helping to create a natural, softer look. Avoid bright, blingy, bold colors, as they can be distracting in photographs. To feel relaxed during your photoshoot, choose lightweight, breathable clothing that is easy to move around. 

Lightweight fabrics like linen, cotton, or chiffon are the best for the beach. Do not wear clothing with busy patterns or logos, as they can also look tacky in photographs. Instead, opt for solid colors or soft patterns like polka dots or stripes.

### Be Flexible

Toddlers can be pretty unpredictable, so be prepared to be flexible and follow the flow during the family photo session. If your toddler is upset or needs a break, take a short break and try again later. Trust me, this short break can bring amazing results for the shoot.

Schedule the photo session when your toddler is rested and well-fed. Avoid having the session during their nap or meal time. They can become really cranky and uncooperative, ruining all the fun from the photo shoot.

### Wear Simple Jewelry

Keep your jewelry simple and understated. Diamonds, gold, or other expensive stones must be avoided as they can easily get lost in the beach scenery. Wear simple earrings or a statement necklace and add a hint to your outfit without distraction.

Instead of jewelry, bring along some props that show your family's personality. This could be anything from a favorite book or old sweater to a toy truck or musical instrument. You can even remember the deceased one in these portraits using their belongings.

### Preparing Your Kids

Getting your kids ready for a family photo shoot could be a headache. However, with a bit of preparation, it can be a fun experience for them as well. 

Tell your children what will happen during the photo shoot and what they can expect. You can say, "This is our photographer, and they will be taking pictures and playing with you.". Let them know that it will be a fun experience, and they will get to play on the beach while having their picture taken.

Kids can get hungry and grumpy really fast. To diffuse this, bring plenty of snacks and drinks to keep them energized during the photoshoot. Bring beach toys, like shovels and buckets, and let them build sandcastles or play in the water.

Dress your kids in breathable clothing that they can move in and that will not cause discomfort or irritation. Make sure they wear shoes they can run around in that will not hurt their feet on the sand.

### Keep it Positive

Keep the mood upbeat, and don't pressure your kids to behave or perform a certain way. Do not ruin the beautiful beach day for them for some photos. Remember, the main goal behind this is to capture jovial memories and have fun on the beach!
