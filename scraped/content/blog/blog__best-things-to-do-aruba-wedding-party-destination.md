Planning a destination wedding in Aruba not only promises stunning landscapes and perfect weather, but it also offers a whole host of activities and experiences for you and your wedding party to enjoy, making it [**_the perfect wedding destination._**](https://www.g10.studio/photography-in-aruba-blog/top-reasons-to-choose-aruba-for-your-perfect-destination-wedding) Here’s a guide to making the most of your time in Aruba with your loved ones, both before and after the big day.

## **Beach Day Extravaganza**

Aruba is renowned for its breathtaking beaches, and what better way to kick off your wedding celebrations than with a beach day? Either arrange for a private beach cabana or rent beach chairs and umbrellas for your group. Spend the day soaking up the sun, swimming in the clear turquoise waters, and enjoying piña coladas delivered right to your spot in the sand. You can even organize beach games like volleyball or paddleboarding for some friendly competition and laughs (just make sure no one gets injured before the wedding).

## **Sunset Sail and Dinner Cruise**

Treat your wedding party to a magical sunset sail and dinner cruise along Aruba’s stunning coastline. Board a luxurious catamaran or yacht with [_Pelican Adventures Aruba_](https://www.pelican-aruba.com/eat-drink/?gad_source=1&gclid=Cj0KCQjwv7O0BhDwARIsAC0sjWPD6lpXgxONEqwEqhcRFvId3mAcRpV5PukMsFbMDvZybAB8RaAZCwsaAr2hEALw_wcB) and set sail just as the sun dips below the horizon, painting the sky in gorgeous hues of orange and pink. Enjoy cocktails and hors d'oeuvres while staring at the sunset, then savor a delicious dinner under the stars. It’s the perfect opportunity to relax, unwind, and catch up with your closest friends and family.

If you want something less formal, you can always book[ _a Jolly Pirates cruise_](https://www.jolly-pirates.com/) \- filled with rope swings, unlimited booze, and good times!

### **Explore Aruba’s Natural Wonders**

A common misconception is that Aruba’s only attraction is beaches. Take my word for it, we have loads of different places for you to explore! For example, you can take your wedding party on an adventure to explore the island’s diverse landscapes. Visit Arikok National Park to discover rugged desert terrain, ancient caves adorned with Arawak petroglyphs, and stunning viewpoints overlooking the coastline. Hike to natural pools where you’ll be able to swim in crystal-clear waters surrounded by dramatic rock formations. It’s the perfect chance to connect with nature and experience the unique charm of Aruba’s wilderness. You can also [**_book an adventure session_**](https://www.g10.studio/photography-in-aruba-blog/why-is-booking-a-post-wedding-adventure-session-with-your-wedding-photographer-a-must) with me, and I’ll take you to some hidden gems on the island.

### **Indulge in Local cuisine and Nightlife**

No destination wedding in Aruba is complete without experiencing its vibrant culinary scene and lively nightlife. Your wedding party can get a taste of Aruba’s flavors at a local restaurant specializing in fresh seafood, Caribbean fusion dishes, or traditional Aruban cuisine like keshi yena (stuffed cheese) and pan bati (cornbread). 

After dinner, head to Palm Beach or downtown Oranjestad for a night of dancing, live music, and cocktails at one of our amazing beach clubs or bars. It’s the ideal way to celebrate love, friendship, and the excitement of your upcoming wedding day. If you’re looking for a dining experience without the cruise part, [_Barefoot_](https://barefootaruba.com/)and [_Flying Fishbone_](https://www.flyingfishbone.com/) offer a fine dining seafood experience that is to die for. On the more casual side, you have [_Zeerovers_](https://www.arubarestaurants.com/restaurant/zeerover/), which are still some of the best fish I’ve had on the island - and that’s saying something! 

### **Relax and rejuvenate**

Wedding planning can be hectic, so you should definitely take some time to relax and rejuvenate with your wedding party. Schedule a spa day at one of Aruba’s luxurious resorts or wellness centers. Treat yourselves to massages, facials, and pampering treatments that will leave you feeling refreshed and ready for your wedding day. Many resorts actually offer special bridal and groom packages, ensuring everyone feels pampered and relaxed before the festivities begin. 

You can also book a day at the [_Renaissance Island_](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/auabr-renaissance-wind-creek-aruba-resort/renaissance-island/), if they have any spots left that is - it tends to get booked up pretty quickly - but it’s definitely still worthwhile to check it out. Or you could treat yourself to some beautiful new jewelry at my family store, [_Noble Jeweler_](https://noblejewelersaruba.com/)s (located in Oranjestad).

### **Water sports and adventure activities**

For the more adventurous members of your wedding party, Aruba offers a host of thrilling water sports and outdoor activities. Go snorkeling or scuba diving to explore vibrant coral reefs teeming with turtles, fish, and so much more! Or perhaps you want to try [_kitesurfing_](https://www.arubakitesurf.com/) in the island’s constant trade winds.

If neither of those sounds good to you, then you can always go offroading (something I always do with either _ABC tours_ or _De Palm Tours_ and highly recommend) or go horseback riding along secluded trails. 

Basically, it doesn’t matter whether you’re seeking adrenaline-pumping adventures or serene moments in nature, because Aruba has something for everyone to enjoy.

### **Fun Extras**

If you, or anyone in the group, is interested in sea life, then Turtle Whisperer Aruba is for you! They offer turtle snorkel tours where you’ll get to see those beautiful creatures up close and personal!! And, in case you’re not done with the ocean just yet (can’t say I blame you), you can look up Aruba Surf and Paddle School, which offers a wide variety of activities - from surfing to fishing, and more!! 

### **Final Thoughts**

Your destination wedding in Aruba is not just about the ceremony and reception. It’s about creating unforgettable memories with your nearest and dearest. From relaxing beach days and sunset cruises to exploring natural wonders and indulging in local cuisine, there is no shortage of Aruban adventures for you and your wedding party to enjoy. 

All I have left to say is... get ready for an unforgettable wedding adventure in paradise!
