## **Top Small Beaches in Aruba for the Perfect Intimate Wedding**

Whether you’re wondering, **"**[**What are the most romantic beaches in Aruba for a wedding**](/blog/what-are-the-most-romantic-beaches-in-aruba-for-wedding-photos)**?** " or looking for a secluded spot for an intimate celebration, this guide will help you find the ideal location. ## **Baby Beach: The Quintessential Romantic Escape**

Baby Beach is a tranquil paradise for intimate weddings. Its calm, shallow lagoon and soft white sand create a serene setting, surrounded by breathtaking turquoise waters—a picture-perfect spot for romance.

### **Why Baby Beach For Your Wedding?**

Baby Beach is not only a stunning but also a romantic location. Its unspoiled beauty and quiet atmosphere make it a great choice for small weddings. Whether you choose a barefoot ceremony right by the water or prefer a secluded spot under the shade of palm trees, Baby Beach’s natural charm sets the perfect tone for a romantic, intimate celebration.

**Photography Tips for Baby Beach**

  * **Early Morning or Late Afternoon:** Capture the soft light during these times for a dreamy, romantic feel.

  * **Pastel Sky Reflections:** Use the reflections in the shallow water to add depth to your photos.

  * **Barefoot Ceremony:** Focus on the serene atmosphere with barefoot shots against the turquoise backdrop.




## **Rodger’s Beach: Serenity and Stunning Views**

Rodger’s Beach is a hidden gem with crystal-clear waters and a peaceful ambiance. Its secluded location ensures a private and romantic setting.

### **Why Choose Rodger’s Beach for Your Wedding**

Rodger’s Beach is ideal for couples who want to be surrounded by the beauty of nature without the interruptions of large crowds. Its stunning, unspoiled coastline makes it an intimate space to exchange vows, and its remote location ensures privacy for your special day.

### **Photography Tips for Rodger’s Beach**

  1. **Golden Hour Shots:** Plan your ceremony during sunset to capture the warm, magical hues.

  2. **Natural Framing:** The surrounding rocks and gentle waves frame intimate moments perfectly.

  3. **Sunset Backdrops:** Incorporate the stunning Caribbean sunset to enhance your wedding portraits.




## **Malmok Beach: A Quiet Paradise for Ceremonies and Snorkeling**

For couples who want to combine their wedding with a bit of adventure, Malmok Beach is an excellent choice. This quieter beach features calm waters and beautiful coral formations just offshore, making it ideal for those who want both an intimate ceremony and an opportunity for some post-wedding snorkeling.

### **Why Malmok Beach Stands Out**

Malmok Beach offers a peaceful atmosphere, and its secluded nature allows for a more personal experience. The rocky coastline adds a dramatic element to the beach, creating a stunning backdrop for your ceremony. Plus, the nearby coral reefs provide an excellent opportunity for exploration after the vows have been exchanged, making this a truly unique and memorable wedding experience.

### **Photography Tips for Malmok Beach**

  * **Underwater Shots:** Take advantage of Malmok's clear waters and vibrant marine life to get some underwater wedding photos.

  * **Rocky Backdrops:** Capture dramatic moments with the rocky coastline behind you.

  * **Post-Wedding Snorkeling:** Use your snorkel adventure as a unique photo opportunity with marine life.




## **Boca Catalina: A Hidden Cove for an Intimate Celebration**

Boca Catalina is a perfect choice if you dream of a secluded and intimate beach wedding. Surrounded by rocky areas teeming with marine life, Boca Catalina's natural beauty will give your wedding a truly unique touch.

### **Why Boca Catalina is Perfect for Your Wedding**

The small size of this beach means that you won’t have to worry about large crowds or distractions. You can simply take in the beauty of nature and your love without the fear of interruption.

### **Photography Tips for Boca Catalina**

  * **Secluded Backdrops:** Frame your photos with the beach’s rocky areas and crystal-clear waters.

  * **Marine Life:** Highlight the surrounding vibrant marine life for an unforgettable, nature-filled wedding album.

  * **Natural Lighting:** Use the natural sunlight filtering through the cove for soft, glowing wedding portraits.




## **Hadicurari Beach (Fisherman’s Huts): A Sunset Lover’s Dream**

Hadicurari Beach is a fantastic spot for couples looking for stunning sunset views. The sunsets at Hadicurari Beach are legendary, painting the sky in shades of pink, orange, and purple, making it a romantic and unforgettable setting for a wedding.

### **Perfect for Couples Who Love Romance and Natural Beauty**

Whether you are planning an early morning ceremony or a sunset celebration, Hadicurari Beach provides an intimate and picturesque location for your vows. Its peaceful environment and beautiful views make it one of the best beaches in Aruba for a private, romantic wedding.

### **Photography Tips for Hadicurari Beach**

  * **Golden Hour Photography:** Take advantage soft, glowing light during sunset for gorgeous, romantic wedding portraits.

  * **Sky Reflections:** Use the sunset’s rich hues to create dreamy reflections in the water.

  * **Romantic Silhouettes:** Frame the two of you as silhouettes against the setting sun for timeless photos.




## **Eagle Beach (Southern End): Quiet Serenity at an Iconic Beach**

While Eagle Beach can get busy in some areas, its quieter southern end offers a peaceful and intimate setting for couples seeking privacy. Complete with stunning white sand and beautiful views of the crystal-clear Caribbean waters.

### **Why Eagle Beach is Still a Great Option for Intimate Weddings**

Eagle Beach is well-known for its iconic Divi Divi trees and its wide shoreline, making it a scenic and romantic choice for intimate weddings. The quiet southern section ensures you can still experience this beach's beauty without the crowds.

### **Photography Tips for Eagle Beach**

  * **Divi Divi Trees:** Use the unique shape of the Divi Divi trees as a natural frame for your photos.

  * **Wide Shoreline Shots:** Capture wide, sweeping shots of the beach, emphasizing its vastness and beauty.

  * **Soft Sand & Water Reflections:** For picturesque images, highlight the contrast between the soft sand and the serene water.




## **Arashi Beach: Relaxed Vibes and Gorgeous Sunsets**

Arashi Beach is known for having fewer crowds than popular beaches like Palm Beach and Eagle Beach, making it a fantastic choice for a private ceremony. It offers stunning sunset views and a peaceful ambiance that allows you to focus entirely on the moment.

### **Why Arashi Beach is Ideal for Private Ceremonies**

With its clear waters and uncrowded space, Arashi Beach provides a simple yet elegant setting for couples who want to feel like they’re the only two people on the island.

### **Photography Tips for Arashi Beach**

  * **Candid Moments:** Capture natural, candid moments with the calm beach and sunset as your backdrop.

  * **Sunset Backdrop:** Plan your ceremony just before sunset for warm, golden wedding portraits.

  * **Quiet Beach Shots:** Embrace the relaxed vibe with intimate, undisturbed moments on the beach.




## **Coconut Palm Shore (Near San Nicolas): A Remote Beach Surrounded by Palm Trees**

For couples seeking a truly remote and private beach experience, Coconut Palm Shore near San Nicolas is an excellent choice. Lined with towering palm trees and soft sand, this beach offers the ultimate tropical vibe. 

### **Why Coconut Palm Shore is Perfect for Intimate Weddings**

Coconut Palm Shore's untouched feel offers a peaceful retreat where couples can focus entirely on each other and their love.

### **Photography Tips for Coconut Palm Shore**

  * **Palm Tree Framing:** Frame your photos with the towering palm trees for a tropical vibe.

  * **Golden Sand Shots:** Capture the contrast between the green palms and golden sand for a vibrant, tropical feel.

  * **Secluded Photos:** Use the remote, untouched nature of the beach for intimate, private wedding photos.
