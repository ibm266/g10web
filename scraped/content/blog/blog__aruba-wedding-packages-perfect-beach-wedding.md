## **What Key Things Should Couples Weigh Before Booking Aruba Wedding Packages?**

Review the venue's vibe, exact inclusions, and legal requirements, such as passports, apostilles, birth certificates, and proof of single status. Submit early since town hall slots fill quickly. Match budget to guest needs, check peak pricing, and confirm resort tie-ins for rooms. This keeps the beach celebration smooth and legal.​

**Overlooking one detail can snag the day, so here's a quick checklist to stay ahead.**

  * Scout beaches: Eagle for divi-divi trees and sunsets; Palm for resort convenience

  * Verify the all-inclusive scope covers chairs for 20, or only the arch?

  * Budget buffer for tips, flights, or divorce decrees if remarrying

  * Legal prep: Two witnesses over 18 with passports, forms apostilled

  * Resort specifics: Packages at The Ritz-Carlton, Marriott, or Renaissance often bundle stays with ceremony perks, but be sure to read the minimum room block requirements.
