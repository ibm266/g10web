### 1\. Renaissance Aruba Resort & Casino: A Fancy and Exotic Wedding Experience

Renaissance Aruba Resort & Casino is an exclusive venue for destinations weddings in Aruba**.** Two love birds can exchange vows by the beach with beautiful birds and luscious greenery as a backdrop. We should also mention that this fabulous hotel features large rooms, an exquisite menu, and a no-children-allowed policy.

Many guests view Flamingo Island as an amazing location in and of itself, which doubles as a beautiful wedding photoshoot location. Plus, the resort's wedding facilities can accommodate your vision and the resort has a dedicated team and staff to make the ceremonies flow as smoothly as possible. For example, a civil wedding can be held in a charming place beside the sea, with a warm sea breeze, soft, sparing light, and a fantastic sunset view.

### 2\. Aruba Marriott Resort & Stellaris Casino: Elegance and Beachfront Charm

This location is perfect for couples who dream of having a beautiful and lavish wedding. It is also an ideal location for any Aruban wedding photographer. It has a beach-front view, a grand ballroom perfect for the reception, and loads of other places outside which will be great for the ceremony. Not to mention, the couple has a variety of wedding packages to select from, including catering services, floral services, and equipment hire for wedding decorations.

As for the other activities, having dinner with guests in one of the resort’s exquisite restaurants or having fun at the casino are just two of the options.

### 3\. Hilton Aruba Caribbean Resort & Casino: A Variety of Ceremony Options

The Hilton Aruba Caribbean Resort & Casino has loads to offer for couples wanting to get married on this tropical paradise. At the Hilton, they also provide a range of options for themes, including beach weddings and garden ceremonies. This resort truly has it all. Couples can see beautiful views and get direct access to the beach. What more could you ask for really? 

As the cherry on top, when the couple has said "I do," all their guests can join them in enjoying the resort services such as spa and food! 

### 4\. The Ritz-Carlton, Aruba: Sophistication & Bespoke Wedding Services

The Ritz Carlton is considered one of the top Aruba wedding locations**.** It is the best venue for couples interested in renting a luxurious space and having an elegant, glamorous wedding event. This five-star wedding resort provides ocean-view weddings and a number of extra wedding services to make the occasion beyond exceptional.

You can choose to have your wedding ceremony outdoors, on beaches, or on a private terrace. Whatever you end up deciding, taking comfort in knowing that all wedding ceremonies are closely coordinated with the resort's professional wedding planners, including flowers, decorations, food and drinks, music, etc. As an exclusive luxury resort and spa, it also provides the best setting for post-wedding experiences as well as top-notch service.

### 5\. Courtyard Aruba Resort: A Less Formalized Experience

The Courtyard by Marriott Aruba Resort is the best choice for couples planning a small or intimate beach wedding. This resort offers an elegant and serene setting, allowing for individual attentiveness and budget-friendly wedding plans and offers.

There are options to have an open-air beach wedding with friends and family or for the bride and groom to be married in a ceremony held in one of the resort's gardens. In both instances, the resort's professional staff ensures all the work gets done, from start to finish. That way, the couple is free from any and all stress during the ceremony.

### 6\. Divi Aruba All-Inclusive: Wedding Planning Made Easy

The Divi Aruba All-Inclusive Resort boasts an all-inclusive Aruban wedding package. This includes catering service, flowers, and a beach event where everything is exclusively arranged.

For the bride and groom, the resort offers a variety of activities, including water sports, food and wine, and lots of dancing.

Overall, since it is family-oriented, the Divi Aruba All-Inclusive knows exactly how to provide a gorgeous wedding for a couple who want zero stress and a good time with their family and friends.

### 7\. Tierra del Sol Resort & Golf: A Memorable Desert Landscape Environment

For couples who want a local desert view and picturesque backdrop, Tierra del Sol Resort & Golf should be the choice. This resort has unique and romantic outside ceremony locations, complete with scenic desert and Aruban backgrounds.

Besides the spectacular scenery, couples can also enjoy a full 18-hole golf course. There are also many facilities that couples and guests can take advantage, like a tennis court, swimming pool, and many restaurants.

### 8\. Hyatt Regency Aruba Resort, Spa, and Casino: A Variety of Wedding Venues

For those looking to book an Aruban wedding, you can find some new and exciting venues in the Hyatt Regency Aruba Resort, Spa, and Casino. If a couple decides to have their ceremony on the sand, they can say their vows on the beach with the blue ocean behind them.

The resort also has several halls to accommodate clients who want an air-conditioned room. As if that wasn’t enough, it also offers spa services, a casino, and other exclusive eateries.
