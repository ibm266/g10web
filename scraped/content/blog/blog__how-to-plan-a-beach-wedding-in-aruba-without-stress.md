### Nail the Legalities Without the Headaches

Let’s get real: no one wants to deal with paperwork, especially when paradise awaits. Thankfully, Aruba’s legal process is fairly sraightforward. While many couples opt for symbolic ceremonies, you can also have a legally binding wedding in Aruba.

**Legal Checklist:**

  * Submit legal documents at least 30 days before the wedding.

  * Required documents:

    * Birth certificates.

    * Passports.

    * Single status declarations.

    * Divorce decrees (if applicable).

  * Civil ceremonies must take place at Oranjestad’s Civil Town Hall.

  * Symbolic ceremonies can be held on the beach.

### Budget Tips for a Stress-Free Wedding

**Key Budget-Saving Tips:**

  * Opt for all-inclusive resort packages to simplify planning.

  * Use local flowers and seasonal decor to reduce costs.

  * Hire vendors experienced with beach weddings to avoid unexpected expenses.

  * Limit the guest list to keep things intimate and affordable.




**Pro Tip:** Allocate extra funds for photography; this is the one element you’ll treasure forever.

### Roll Out the Red Carpet for Your Guests

Your guests are taking a trip to celebrate you, so make it an experience they’ll rave about for years to come. From curated welcome bags with sunscreen and flip-flops to arranging group activities like snorkeling or catamaran tours, show them Aruba through your eyes. If you can, try to plan your wedding so that each guest leaves with laughter and memories they’ll never forget. After all, the guests make the wedding what it is! 

### Have a Plan B (Because Mother Nature Runs the Show)

Aruba’s weather is famously sunny, but don’t tempt fate. Choose a venue with nearby indoor or tented options. Of course rain might create unexpected memories, just make sure you’re prepared so that they’re good ones.

**Tips for Weatherproofing:**

  * Provide shade with tents or umbrellas.

  * Offer water stations and hand fans to guests.

  * Use wind-resistant fabrics for decor.

  * Have a resort or indoor backup location.




### Keep the Decor Beach-Friendly

When your backdrop is the sparkling Caribbean Sea, less is more. Choose decor that complements the natural beauty. Think breezy fabrics, local flowers, and subtle accents. Imagine how string lights swaying in the breeze or simple bamboo chairs can elevate the vibe without overpowering the scenery.
