## **Where Is Aruba Located?**

Aruba is a small island in the Caribbean Sea. It lies just north of Venezuela. The place belongs to the Kingdom of the Netherlands. Moreover, it mixes desert landscapes with clean beaches and lively local culture. The island is known for its year-round warm, sunny weather. That turns it into a great choice for weddings and photos.

This island is about 15 miles off the coast of Venezuela. This spot makes travel simple from many areas of the United States. It is 20 miles long. But it only reaches 6 miles across at its widest. Aruba works well for couples looking to share quality moments.

**Quick Facts About Aruba:**

  * **Is Aruba in the Caribbean?** Yes, Aruba is part of the Caribbean islands.

  * **How big is Aruba?** The [Aruba island](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aruba) measures 20 miles long and 6 miles wide.

## **FAQs**

### **What’s the best time to capture wedding photos in Aruba?**

The best time to capture wedding photos in Aruba is early morning or late afternoon, when the lighting is soft and flattering. The dry season, from January to March, offers the most consistent sunny weather for outdoor photography.

### **Which beaches in Aruba offer the top settings for wedding photography?**

Aruba boasts several stunning beaches for wedding photos, including Flamingo Beach for its tranquility, Moomba Beach for vibrant shots, and Aruba Eagle Beach, famous for its iconic divi-divi trees and clear waters.

### **How can couples prepare for their Aruba destination wedding photo session?**

Couples should plan ahead by discussing location preferences with their photographer, choosing breathable outfits for comfort in Aruba’s heat, and scheduling shoots during golden hour for the best lighting.

### **Why is Aruba a standout destination for dream wedding photos?**

Aruba stands out due to its beautiful beaches, year-round sunny weather, and stunning landscapes. It offers diverse backdrops for wedding photos, from serene beaches to rugged terrains, making it an ideal choice.

### **How far in advance should photography be booked for a destination wedding in Aruba?**

It’s recommended to book your photographer 6–12 months in advance, especially for weddings in peak season (January to March). Aruba’s popularity means that top photographers often book out early.
