# How Much Should You Budget for Wedding Photography in Aruba?

Dec 17

Written By [Jiten Melwani](/blog?author=62ed087ff4f34a1415eacc32)

Planning a destination wedding in Aruba involves a lot of decision making, and one of the most important is your photography budget. [**Wedding photography in Aruba** ](/photography/wedding-in-aruba)is not just about capturing beautiful moments; it’s about preserving memories of a day you’ll cherish forever. But how much should you budget for it? 

In this blog, we’ll break down the costs associated with hiring a wedding photographer in Aruba, explore factors that influence pricing, and provide tips for maximizing value without compromising on quality.

### 1\. Average Cost of Wedding Photography in Aruba (approximate prices)

The cost of wedding photography in Aruba varies based on several factors, such as the photographer’s experience, the package you choose, and the level of customization you want. On average:

  * **Basic Packages:** Start at $1,500 and include a few hours of coverage and basic editing.

  * **Mid-Tier Packages:** Range between $3,000 and $5,000, offering full-day coverage, multiple locations, and enhanced editing.

  * **Premium Packages:** Can exceed $8,000, including luxury add-ons like drone photography, custom albums, and multiple photographers.




These prices can fluctuate based on demand, particularly during peak wedding seasons.

### 2\. Factors Influencing Photography Costs 

Several factors contribute to the overall cost of wedding photography in Aruba. Understanding these can help you allocate your budget more effectively.

#### Photographer’s Experience

Experienced photographers with a strong portfolio and years of expertise often charge higher rates. Their familiarity with Aruba’s unique lighting and landscapes ensures top-notch results.

#### Duration of Coverage

The number of hours your photographer spends at your wedding significantly impacts the cost. Full-day coverage will naturally cost more than if you only want them there for a few hours.

#### Travel and Accommodation

If you’re bringing in a photographer from outside Aruba, you’ll need to cover their travel and accommodation expenses. Hiring a local photographer can help reduce these costs as well as help boost Aruba’s economy!

#### Post-Production and Editing

High-quality editing and retouching take time and expertise. Photographers who offer advanced post-production services often charge more for their packages.

### 3\. What’s Included in Wedding Photography Packages?

Wedding photography packages in Aruba can vary widely, so it’s essential to know what’s included. Here’s what you might expect:

#### Basic Inclusions

  * A set number of hours of coverage.

  * Digital copies of edited photos.

  * An online gallery for sharing with friends and family.




#### Mid-Tier Inclusions

  * Multiple locations for photoshoots.

  * A second shooter for additional angles.

  * Prints or a custom wedding album.




#### Premium Add-Ons

  * Drone photography for stunning aerial views.

  * Engagement or post-wedding sessions.

  * Expedited delivery of edited photos.




### 4\. Tips for Budgeting for Wedding Photography

Getting the best value for your budget doesn’t mean compromising on quality. 

Here are some tips:

#### Prioritize Photography

Photography is one of the few investments from your wedding day that lasts forever. Allocate a significant portion of your budget to ensure you hire a professional who meets your expectations and will work to make your vision come to life.

#### Book Early

Aruba’s top photographers are often booked well in advance, especially during peak wedding seasons. Booking early will ensure that you secure your preferred photographer and may even save you money.

#### Customize Your Package

Work with your photographer to tailor a package that meets your needs without exceeding your budget. For example, opt for fewer coverage hours or skip unnecessary add-ons.

#### Consider Off-Peak Seasons

Weddings during Aruba’s off-peak season may come with discounted rates. Discuss this option with your photographer. Off-peak is usually around the Christmas season, since hotel rates are higher and Aruba generally becomes more of a family vacation destination. Weddings tend to happen most when hotel rates are lower during the summer months.

### 5\. Balancing Cost and Quality

While it’s tempting to choose the most affordable option, remember that wedding photography is an investment. Balancing cost and quality ensures you get beautiful photos without breaking the bank.

#### Ask for Recommendations

Local wedding planners and venues often have trusted photographers they recommend. These professionals typically have experience working all around Aruba and offer competitive pricing.

#### Review Portfolios Carefully

Always review a photographer’s portfolio to ensure their style aligns with your vision. A lower price may not be worth it if the quality doesn’t meet your expectations. Remember, you want to be happy with the final product once the day is over.

#### Plan for Hidden Costs

Be aware of potential hidden costs, such as additional editing, travel fees, or extended coverage. Discuss these details upfront to avoid surprises.

### 6\. Alternatives to Traditional Wedding Photography

If you’re working with a tight budget, consider alternative options:

#### Hire a Rising Talent

Up-and-coming photographers may offer lower rates while still delivering excellent results. Check their portfolios to ensure quality.

#### Limit Coverage Time

Focus on key moments of your wedding day, such as the ceremony and portraits, rather than booking full-day coverage.

#### DIY Approach

For casual weddings, consider using high-quality smartphones or enlisting a friend to take photos. However, it’s important to keep in mind that this may not provide the professional results you desire.

### 7\. The Value of Local Photographers

Hiring a local photographer in Aruba comes with several advantages:

#### Cost Savings

Local photographers don’t require travel and accommodation expenses, making them a cost-effective choice.

#### Expert Knowledge

They’re familiar with the best photo locations, lighting conditions, and weather patterns of our island, therefore ensuring stunning results.

#### Cultural Understanding

Local photographers often have a deep understanding of Aruba’s culture, which can add a unique touch to your wedding photos.

### 8\. Why Wedding Photography Is Worth the Investment

Your wedding photos are more than just pictures; they’re a visual story of one of the most important days of your life. Investing in high-quality photography means:

  * You’ll have cherished memories to look back on for years to come.

  * Every detail, emotion, and moment is captured beautifully.

  * Your photos reflect the unique essence of your wedding in Aruba.




### Conclusion

Budgeting for wedding photography in Aruba requires thoughtful planning, but it’s definitely worth the time. By understanding the costs, prioritizing quality, and working with a skilled local photographer, you can ensure your wedding photos not only meet but exceed your expectations.
