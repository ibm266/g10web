### **Photography Packages in Aruba: What’s Included?**

Most photographers offer different photography packages in Aruba based on your needs. Standard packages may include:

  * **Basic Package ($250-$500):** 30-minute session, 10-20 edited photos, digital delivery.

  * **Standard Package ($500-$1,200):** 1-2 hours of shooting, 50+ edited images, outfit changes, beach or city location.

  * **Luxury Package ($2,000+):** Full-day coverage, multiple locations, drone photography, custom albums, and more.




### **Aruba Professional Photography Fees: What Influences the Cost?**

Several factors impact Aruba professional photography fees, including:

  * **Experience & Reputation:** Seasoned photographers with awards or extensive portfolios charge higher rates.

  * **Location & Travel:** Sessions held at more remote or exclusive locations may require additional travel fees.

  * **Editing & Retouching:** Basic edits are usually included, but advanced retouching may cost extra.

  * **Extras:** Drone photography, videography, and printed albums often add to the final cost.
