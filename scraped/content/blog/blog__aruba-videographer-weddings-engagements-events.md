### **Following Professional Industry Rules for Better Results**

Experts often use the 30/5 rule to manage the busy timeline of a wedding day. This means adding 30 minutes of buffer for major events and 5 minutes for every person in group shots. Additionally, the 20-60-20 rule in photography helps structure the visual weight of each frame. By placing the subject in the central 60% and using the remaining 40% for the stunning Aruban landscape, the film feels balanced and professional.
