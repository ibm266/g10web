# Sunrise vs sunset photography session for your vacation photos on one happy island Aruba?

Written By [Jiten Melwani](/blog?author=62ed087ff4f34a1415eacc32)

As a local-turned-photographer, I have the privilege of capturing the island's natural beauty and I can confidently say, even after 6 years of photography, it never disappoints. That being said, one question that often arises when planning a photoshoot in Aruba is whether to schedule it during the golden hour of sunrise or the magical moments of sunset. In this blog post, I'll give you the inside scoop on both options so that you can make an informed decision (or just book two shoots for both times!)

### **Sunset Photoshoots**

**Golden Hour Glow:** Sunset photoshoots in Aruba offer a warm, golden glow that bathes the landscape in soft, flattering light. This magical hour provides the perfect backdrop for romantic portraits and breathtaking landscapes. Not to mention, even when it's cloudy out, the red sky is still a stunning sight.

**Vibrant Colors:** The vibrant hues of the setting sun create a stunning palette of colors, ranging from fiery oranges and pinks to deep purples and blues. Sunset photoshoots in Aruba can capture the island's vibrant allure in all its glory.

**Romantic Atmosphere:** There's something undeniably romantic about watching the sun dip below the horizon, making sunset photoshoots the perfect choice for couples looking to capture the essence of love and romance in their images. Lucky for you, I actually specialize in wedding photography!

### **Sunrise Photoshoots**

**Peaceful Atmosphere:** Sunrise photoshoots offer a sense of tranquility and serenity as the world awakens to a new day. The calmness of the early morning hours creates a peaceful atmosphere that is perfect for intimate portraits and reflective moments. I would usually recommend this time for family shoots because the colors of the ocean and the sky really pop in pictures.

**Soft Light:** The light during sunrise is soft and diffused, creating a flattering glow that enhances skin tones and reduces harsh shadows. This gentle light is ideal for capturing natural, ethereal images with a dreamy quality.

**Unique Perspective:** Sunrise photoshoots offer a unique perspective of Aruba's landscapes, with the soft morning light casting a magical glow over the island's iconic landmarks and natural wonders. It truly feels like you are one with the nature around you.

### **Conclusion**

Whether you choose a sunset or sunrise photoshoot, both offer unique benefits and will invariably create unforgettable memories. No matter what, I am dedicated to ensuring that we work together to help bring your vision to life. This means that, whichever you choose I can guarantee that your photoshoot in Aruba is sure to be a magical experience.
