### **What’s usually included in proposal packages at Aruba resorts?**

Most proposal packages at Aruba resorts cover location setup, basic decor, and timed photography. Many add champagne, flowers, or musician options. Confirm the exact minutes of coverage, delivery timelines, and any additional resort fees for external photographers before booking.

### **Is engagement photography at the Ritz-Carlton Aruba different from other resorts?**

Often, yes. Engagement photography at the Ritz-Carlton Aruba benefits from premium spaces, better privacy, and attentive staff coordination. You get polished settings and smoother logistics. That helps the photographer work more efficiently and keep you relaxed during the golden hour.

### **When is the best time of day for proposal photos in Aruba?**

Golden hour wins. For luxury resort photography in Aruba, sunrise gives empty beaches and soft pastels. Sunset brings a warm glow and dramatic skies. Midday works only with shade or terraces. Trust your photographer’s spot list for clean light and easy posing.

### **How far in advance should I book a proposal photography session?**

For proposal photography at Aruba resorts, aim for a duration of 4 to 8 weeks. Popular dates and sunset slots fill fast. If you want custom decor or a musician, add more lead time. Share your resort confirmation early so your photographer can secure permits if needed.

### **Do resorts allow outside photographers for proposal shoots?**

Many do, but policies vary. Check with your venue before booking. Some proposal packages at Aruba resorts include in-house shooters, while others allow outside artists with approval. Share your photographer’s details early to ensure security and access remain smooth.
