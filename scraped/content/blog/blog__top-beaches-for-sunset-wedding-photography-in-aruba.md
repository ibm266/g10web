# Top Beaches for Sunset Wedding Photography in Aruba 

Jan 14

Written By [Jiten Melwani](/blog?author=62ed087ff4f34a1415eacc32)

If you’ve ever dreamed of exchanging vows with a stunning golden sky as your backdrop, Aruba’s beaches are the ultimate setting for a breathtaking sunset wedding. The island’s serene coastline offers countless spots where the golden hour can make your ceremony magical. But that begs the question, which beach is the best for sunset****[**wedding photos in Aruba**](/photography/wedding-in-aruba)**?** Whether you’re imagining a private ceremony with your toes in the sand or a more luxurious venue with panoramic views, Aruba has the perfect location to make your sunset dreams come true. 

From the iconic Palm Beach to more secluded hideaways, let’s explore the top beaches on the island where the sun will set on your new beginning with unforgettable beauty.

## **Top Beaches for Wedding Photography in Aruba**

Now that we’re all on the same page re: the appeal of a sunset wedding in Aruba let’s look at some of the island’s most popular beaches for a golden hour wedding in Aruba.

### 1\. Eagle Beach: The Iconic Sunset Spot

#### **Why Choose Eagle Beach?**

Eagle Beach is one of Aruba’s most popular beaches for a reason. It genuinely offers everything you could want for a sunset wedding: vast stretches of soft white sand, crystal-clear waters, and iconic Divi-Divi trees. Its unobstructed view of the horizon provides the perfect backdrop for a wedding ceremony, with the sun sinking into the ocean in full view. Eagle Beach is also famous for its tranquility and beauty. Essentially, it’s no wonder why couples choose this beautiful beach to say their vows. 

#### What Makes It Special?

  * **Unmatched Views:** Eagle Beach is one of the most expansive beaches in Aruba, offering plenty of space for your wedding setup. Whether you’re having an intimate ceremony with just your closest family or a grand event with 100+ guests, the vast open space can accommodate any vision.

  * **Iconic Divi-Divi Trees:** These unique trees lean toward the ocean, adding an exotic, photo-worthy element to your ceremony. They’re often featured in wedding photos and help make your sunset wedding truly memorable.

  * **Romantic Sunsets:** The unobstructed sunset views are a huge selling point of****[**romantic beach weddings in Aruba**](/blog/what-are-the-most-romantic-beaches-in-aruba-for-wedding-photos)**.** The sky seems to explode with color as the sun dips into the Caribbean Sea, providing an awe-inspiring atmosphere which is simply perfect for your big moment.




**Best for:** Couples looking for a classic beach wedding with plenty of room for their guests.

### 2\. Palm Beach: Vibrant and Lively Vibes

#### Why Choose Palm Beach?

If you want a sunset wedding in Aruba but still want to feel the vibrancy of the island, Palm Beach is a fantastic choice. Known for its upscale resorts, lively beach bars, and beautiful views, Palm Beach offers an exciting backdrop to your ceremony while maintaining the beauty and elegance of a sunset wedding.

#### What Makes It Special?

  * **Vibrant Atmosphere:** Palm Beach is a bit more lively than some of Aruba’s other beaches, which means it’s perfect for couples who want a wedding that combines romance with a sense of celebration. Not to mention, the resorts here offer luxury and convenience, and the nearby restaurants and bars provide ample options for post-wedding festivities.

  * **Perfect for Sunset Photos:** Palm Beach is well-known for its dramatic sunsets, which can be enjoyed from any part of the beach. The view of the sun setting behind the high-rise resorts makes for some incredible photo opportunities.

  * **Convenience:** Being one of the island’s most popular beaches, Palm Beach is easily accessible, with nearby accommodations, restaurants, and resorts. Your guests will have a wide range of lodging, dining, and entertainment options both during the wedding celebrations and in between, ensuring that everyone makes the most of their time on the island.




**Best for:** Couples who want an energetic, celebratory atmosphere with their sunset wedding.

### 3\. Renaissance Island: Private Island Perfection

#### **Why Choose Renaissance Island?**

Renaissance Island is an extraordinary choice for couples who are looking for exclusivity and privacy. Accessible by water taxi, this private island offers seclusion, natural beauty, and intimate settings—perfect for a sunset wedding with just your closest friends and family.

#### What Makes It Special?

  * **Flamingo Beach:** One of the standout features of Renaissance Island is its famous Flamingo Beach, a standout choice for [**sunset photography in Aruba**](/blog/top-beaches-for-sunset-wedding-photography-in-aruba)**,** where wild flamingos roam free. If you’re looking for a unique, whimsical touch to your sunset wedding, the presence of flamingos will provide just that.

  * **Seclusion:** If you want a private experience, Renaissance Island is hard to beat. This exclusive venue ensures you and your guests enjoy a truly intimate wedding entirely away from the crowds.

  * **Unbeatable Views:** The sunsets here are second to none. With the island’s lush landscapes and turquoise waters, the sky becomes a canvas of vibrant hues as the sun sets, providing a breathtaking backdrop for your wedding photos.




**Best for:** Couples seeking a wholly private and intimate sunset wedding.

### 4\. Manchebo Beach: Quiet and Intimate

**Why Choose Manchebo Beach?** For couples who prefer a quieter, more intimate setting, Manchebo Beach is an excellent choice. Located on the western side of Aruba, this beach offers true tranquility and seclusion, perfect for couples who want to exchange vows in peace while enjoying the natural beauty of a sunset over the Caribbean Sea.

#### What Makes It Special?

  * **Serene Atmosphere:** Manchebo Beach is much less crowded than some of Aruba’s other popular beaches, giving you and your guests plenty of space to relax and enjoy the moment. This calm vibe is perfect for a romantic and intimate ceremony.

  * **Golden Hour Beauty:** The sunsets here are awe-inspiring, with soft hues of orange, pink, and purple casting a golden glow over the sand. The intimate, peaceful setting also makes it ideal for a quiet sunset ceremony.

  * **Boutique Vibe:** The boutique resorts nearby offer a cozy and romantic atmosphere that enhances the intimate feeling of your wedding. The smaller crowds ensure that your day feels exclusive and personal.




**Best for:** Couples looking for a more intimate, low-key sunset wedding.

### 5\. Arashi Beach: Peaceful and Picturesque

**Why Choose Arashi Beach?** Arashi Beach is the perfect choice if you’re searching for a quiet and more peaceful location for your sunset wedding. Located on the northern tip of Aruba, this beach offers a pristine and natural setting, generally with fewer crowds and plenty of space for your ceremony.

**What Makes It Special?**

  * **Natural Beauty:** Arashi Beach is known for its untouched beauty, with clear waters, soft sand, and stunning views of the Caribbean Sea. It’s the perfect place for couples who want a more natural, laid-back wedding.

  * **Quiet and Tranquil:** Arashi Beach is less crowded than other beaches on the island, providing a serene setting for couples who want to enjoy their ceremony without any distractions. And, as an added bonus, the natural landscape is the perfect canvas for your wedding photos.

  * **Stunning Sunsets:** With unobstructed views of the horizon, the sunsets here are truly spectacular. As the sun sets over the water, it creates the perfect lighting for wedding photos that will be treasured for a lifetime.




**Best for** Couples seeking a tranquil, peaceful sunset wedding with a stunning natural backdrop.

### Conclusion:

When planning a sunset wedding in Aruba, choosing which beach to go for is a difficult decision. Especially since each beach has its unique charm, and the best one for you depends on the atmosphere you want to create. Whether you choose the lively Palm Beach or the intimate and serene Manchebo Beach, you will invariably find a stunning backdrop for your special day.

For couples who want privacy, Renaissance Island offers an exclusive experience, while Eagle Beach provides classic beauty and unforgettable sunset views. And if you prefer something quieter and more peaceful, Arashi Beach and Manchebo Beach are excellent choices for a romantic sunset wedding.
