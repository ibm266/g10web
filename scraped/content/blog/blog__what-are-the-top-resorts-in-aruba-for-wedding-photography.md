##  Top Resorts in Aruba for Wedding Photography

An unforgettable experience is the combination of a lot of different factors. ### 1\. The Ritz-Carlton, Aruba

  * **Stunning Beachfront Location:** The Ritz-Carlton is on pristine Palm Beach, offering a gorgeous backdrop of white sands and turquoise waters to **capture the perfect beach wedding photos in Aruba.** Not to mention, the beach is ideal for romantic sunset photos.

  * **Luxurious Amenities:** This resort boasts elegant ballrooms as well as outdoor venues, including a beachfront gazebo, which can be beautifully decorated for ceremonies. The lavish interiors provide a sophisticated setting for indoor photography.

  * **Professional Services:** The Ritz-Carlton offers wedding planning services, ensuring every detail is handled with precision. Their experienced staff can help coordinate with photographers to capture the best moments of your wedding day.

  * **Gorgeous Gardens:** The resort features beautifully landscaped gardens, providing additional picturesque spots for wedding photos. The combination of tropical flora and elegant architecture creates a beautiful romantic atmosphere.




### 2\. Hyatt Regency Aruba Resort Spa and Casino

  * **Diverse Photography Locations:** The Hyatt Regency of [**Aruba is one of the best for a Sunset Wedding**](/blog/top-beaches-for-sunset-wedding-photography-in-aruba) and offers a variety of stunning locations for wedding photography, from its lush gardens to the expansive beachfront. The resort's pools and waterfalls also provide unique backdrops for some more creative shots.

  * **Elegant Indoor Spaces:** For couples looking for indoor options, the resort features beautifully designed ballrooms with large windows that allow natural light to flood in, perfect for capturing every single moment.

  * **Sunset Views:** The resort's beachfront location lets you capture breathtaking sunset photos outside your venue. And, always remember, the golden hour light enhances the beauty of your wedding photographs.

  * **On-Site Wedding Coordination:** The Hyatt Regency has a dedicated wedding team that can assist with planning and coordinating your special day, ensuring that everything runs smoothly and that your photographer has access to the best spots.




### 3\. Manchebo Beach Resort & Spa

  * **Intimate and Romantic Setting:** Manchebo Beach Resort is a boutique property with a more intimate atmosphere, perfect for smaller weddings. The resort's beachfront location provides a stunning backdrop for wedding photos.

  * **Wellness-Focused Experience:** The resort's focus on wellness and relaxation adds a unique touch to your wedding experience. Couples can enjoy yoga sessions on the beach, which can be beautifully captured in photographs.

  * **Charming Decor:** The resort's charming decor and laid-back vibe create a relaxed atmosphere for wedding photography. Plus, the thatched-roof lobby and open-air spaces provide a unique aesthetic.

  * **Private Beach Access:** With fewer crowds, couples can enjoy a more private beach experience, allowing uninterrupted photography sessions and stunning shots without distractions.

### 4\. Renaissance Wind Creek Aruba Resort

  * **Private Island Experience:** The Renaissance Resort features a private island, otherwise known as Flamingo Beach, accessible only by water taxi. This exclusive location offers pristine beaches and secluded spots for breathtaking wedding photos.

  * **Flamingo Beach:** The resort's Flamingo Beach is famous for its resident flamingos, providing a unique and whimsical backdrop for wedding photography. Imagine capturing your special moments with these beautiful birds in the background!

  * **Diverse Venues:** The resort offers various indoor and outdoor venues, including elegant ballrooms and beachfront locations, allowing couples to choose the perfect setting for their ceremony and reception.

  * **Vibrant Downtown Location:** Located in Oranjestad, the resort is close to colorful Dutch colonial buildings, providing additional opportunities for lively and culturally rich wedding photography.

  * **Casual and Relaxed Atmosphere:** The Divi Aruba's laid-back vibe allows for a more relaxed wedding experience, which can be beautifully captured in candid photography.

  * **On-Site Wedding Coordination:** The resort provides on-site wedding coordination services, ensuring that all the wedding admin is handled. This allows couples to simply enjoy their day and focus on capturing memories.




### 6\. Hilton Aruba Caribbean Resort & Casino

  * **Iconic Palm Beach Location:** The Hilton Aruba is located on the famous Palm Beach, providing a stunning backdrop for wedding photography. The resort's expansive beachfront also allows for beautiful sunset shots.

## Tips for Capturing Stunning Photos at Aruba Resorts

#### **1\. Use the Resort’s Architecture:**

  * Highlight grand entrances, columns, and terraces for elegant shots.

  * Take photos in front of fountains and pools for reflections.




#### **2\. Focus on Natural Elements:**

#### **3\. Golden Hour Photography:**
