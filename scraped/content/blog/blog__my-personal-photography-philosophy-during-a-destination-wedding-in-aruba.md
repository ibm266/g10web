# My personal photography philosophy during a destination wedding in Aruba 

[Destination Wedding](/blog/category/Destination+Wedding)[Aruba](/blog/category/Aruba)[Aruba Weddings](/blog/category/Aruba+Weddings)[Wedding Photography](/blog/category/Wedding+Photography)[Wedding Planning](/blog/category/Wedding+Planning)

Written By [Jiten Melwani](/blog?author=62ed087ff4f34a1415eacc32)

Wedding photographers hold the entire operation together. From start to finish, we are there, making sure every moment is captured and everyone is having a good time. For this reason, and the ones discussed below, it’s so important to have a photographer who can maintain a good energy and vibe throughout the entire day. Keep reading to find out more!

**Creating a Comfortable Atmosphere**

As soon as I step into your bridal suite or hotel room, my goal is to create an enjoyable atmosphere. Being charismatic helps me connect with you and your bridal party quickly, making everyone feel at ease. That way, the photography doesn’t feel like just a tick off the list, but a fun experience that helps to calm nerves. Not to mention, when you’re comfortable, your genuine emotions and natural beauty shine through, resulting in stunning, authentic photos.

**Breaking the ice**

Let’s face it, not everyone loves being in front of the camera. A good conversationalist can break the ice and get rid of any camera shyness. By chatting, sharing a few anecdotes, and being genuinely interested in your story, I help you and your guests feel more relaxed. This leads to candid, heartfelt photos that will reflect the true essence of your wedding day.

**Keeping the energy up**

Weddings can often be long days, and maintaining high energy throughout the entire day is essential. A charismatic photographer brings a positive vibe that keeps spirits high, even during quieter moments or unexpected delays. This is especially important when you’re having your wedding day in the[ sunny paradise of Aruba](https://www.g10.studio/photography-in-aruba-blog/top-reasons-to-choose-aruba-for-your-perfect-destination-wedding). We don’t allow any bad vibes around here!! Whether it’s getting everyone excited for group shots or bringing a smile to your face during a lull, my energy sets the tone for a joyful and memorable day. You can rest assured that there will never be a dull moment when I’m around.

**Enhancing the guest experience**

Your guests are a big part of your wedding day, and their experience matters too. A photographer who can engage with guests, remember names, and make them feel included adds to the overall enjoyment of your celebration. It’s about more than just taking photos; it’s about being a part of the day and enhancing the experience for everyone involved. I can’t tell you the number of comments I get from guests thanking me for including them in the celebration, and that’s something I don’t intend on changing. 

**Handling the unexpected**

Weddings are full of surprises, and sometimes things don’t go exactly as planned. A charismatic and adaptable photographer can handle unexpected situations with grace and a positive attitude. Whether it’s a sudden rain shower, a wardrobe malfunction, or a scheduling hiccup, staying calm and cheerful helps keep the day on track and ensures you stay stress-free. That’s exactly why [I always have a plan B](https://www.g10.studio/photography-in-aruba-blog/my-backup-plan-as-a-luxury-destination-wedding-photographer-in-aruba), so that everyone can go into the day with a peaceful mind.

**Capturing authentic moments**

The best wedding photos are those that capture real, unfiltered moments of joy, love, and connection throughout the day. When you’re comfortable with your photographer, these moments happen naturally. My ability to engage with you and your guests, make everyone laugh, and create a fun atmosphere leads to genuine smiles, heartfelt tears, and beautiful interactions captured in your wedding album.

**Final Thoughts**

Choosing a wedding photographer is about more than just their portfolio; it’s about finding someone who brings positive energy, charisma, and a genuine love for what they do. In fact, I am fortunate enough to have a number of clients who have become friends after shooting their weddings because I’m all about having a good time and making sure your wedding is a memorable experience for all parties involved.
