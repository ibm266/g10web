Planning a destination wedding in Aruba is the dream for many couples seeking sun-kissed beaches, azure waters, and romantic sunsets. As a luxury destination photographer, I've had the privilege of capturing countless love stories against the backdrop of this paradise a.k.a. home. In this blog post, I'll share a few expert tips and insider knowledge to help you plan your fairytale island wedding.

## **Choose the Perfect Venue:**

Selecting the right venue is key since it will be the heart of the wedding. Whether you envision an intimate beachfront ceremony, a luxurious resort celebration, or a romantic garden affair, Aruba offers a variety of stunning venues to suit every style and budget. My biggest piece of advice would be to consider factors such as location, amenities, and guest accommodations when choosing the perfect setting for your special day.

## **Timing is Everything:**

Aruba enjoys beautiful weather year-round. In other words, the sunny skies and warm temperatures make it an ideal destination for weddings at any time of the year. However, you might want to think about factors like peak tourist seasons, hurricane season, and local events when choosing your wedding date. Booking your wedding during the island's quieter months, i.e., summer, can help ensure a more intimate and personalized experience for you and your guests.

### **Work with Local Vendors:**

Partnering with local vendors who are familiar with the ins and outs of Aruba can make planning your destination wedding a breeze. The island boasts a wealth of talented professionals who are dedicated to bringing your wildest dreams to life, including experienced wedding planners and talented florists to skilled caterers and professional photographers (like myself!). On this front, be sure to research and connect with vendors who share your style and vision for your wedding day. It’s also worth noting that if you book with me, you’ll have connections to loads of different vendors!

### **Embrace Island Elegance:**

Aruba's natural beauty and laid-back charm provide the perfect backdrop for an elegant and timeless wedding celebration. Embrace the island's relaxed vibe by incorporating local elements such as tropical flowers, vibrant colors, and Caribbean-inspired cuisine into your wedding decor and menu. Whether you opt for a barefoot beach ceremony or a glamorous sunset soiree, you can never go wrong by infusing your wedding with a bit of island elegance.

### **Capture Every Moment:**

I completely understand the importance of preserving your wedding day memories for years to come. That’s actually why I got into photography! So, I feel confident in telling you to ensure that you invest in professional photography services that can capture every magical moment without missing a beat.

Side note: A skilled photographer should be able to capture your love story while also showcasing the beauty of Aruba's landscapes and the emotions shared on your special day.

Ultimately, planning your ideal wedding is an exciting and rewarding journey that requires careful thought, preparation, and attention to detail. By following these expert tips and working with experienced professionals, you can create the nuptials of your dreams on our “One Happy Island”. From selecting the perfect venue to capturing every moment through stunning photography, your Aruban wedding is going to be an unforgettable celebration!
