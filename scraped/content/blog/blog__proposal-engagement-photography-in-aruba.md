## **Best Locations for Proposal and Engagement Photography in Aruba**

When couples search where are the best spots for a proposal in Aruba, the same beaches appear on your screen. But as someone who spends countless hours scouting the island can only confirm there are hidden gems worth considering.

### **Classic Beaches**

  * **Eagle Beach** : Known for its wide shoreline and iconic Divi Divi trees, perfect for a golden-hour proposal.

  * **Manchebo Beach** : Quieter and romantic, ideal for sunset photos with fewer crowds.

  * **Baby Beach** : Crystal-clear shallow waters and palm-framed views for a tropical fairytale.




### **Hidden Gems**

  * **Arashi Beach** : A quieter spot with dunes and rugged beauty, perfect for couples who want privacy.

  * **California Lighthouse cliffs** : Sweeping panoramic views, dramatic light, and a sense of adventure.

  * **Alto Vista Chapel** : A historic, spiritual landmark surrounded by desert landscapes for a unique backdrop.




If your session falls during high tourist season or when cruise ships are in port, plan to arrive early in the morning or on weekdays to avoid the heavy crowds.
