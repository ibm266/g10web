# My wedding photography style: Candid photography in Aruba

[Destination Wedding](/blog/category/Destination+Wedding)[Aruba](/blog/category/Aruba)[Wedding Photography](/blog/category/Wedding+Photography)[Photographer](/blog/category/Photographer)[Candid Photography](/blog/category/Candid+Photography)

Written By [Jiten Melwani](/blog?author=62ed087ff4f34a1415eacc32)

As a photographer who is lucky enough to call Aruba home, I want to take a moment to share with you what sets my photography style apart: candid wedding photos. This isn’t just about snapping pictures; it’s about capturing the genuine moments that tell your unique love story and, by extension, your wedding day.

## **Capturing authentic emotions**

When I think about the weddings I’ve had the honor to photograph here in Aruba, it’s the candid moments that stand out the most. There’s something magical about capturing a stolen glance between the bride and groom during the ceremony or the heartfelt laughter shared between friends during the reception. These are the moments that truly reflect the love, joy, and excitement of bringing your nearest and dearest together to celebrate your love. 

## **Finding beauty in the unexpected**

One of the things I love most about candid photography is its ability to capture beauty in unexpected places. It’s not always about perfectly posed shots (though those have their place, too!). It’s about capturing the little details: the way your hands naturally intertwine, the genuine smiles exchanged between family members, or the quiet moments of reflection amidst the bustling celebrations. These are the moments that make your wedding day uniquely yours, and the ones that you’ll end up remembering years later. Keep in mind, this isn’t limited to the wedding day, but to[ all photography sessions you book with me! ](https://www.g10.studio/photography-in-aruba-blog/why-is-booking-a-post-wedding-adventure-session-with-your-wedding-photographer-a-must)

### **Creating a relaxed atmosphere**

I believe that the best candid photos happen when you and your loved ones feel comfortable and at ease. That’s why I strive to create a relaxed atmosphere where you can be yourselves. This includes cracking a joke here and there as well as striking up a conversation with you and your guests. Whether it’s capturing the excitement of getting ready with your bridal party, the emotional exchange of vows during the ceremony, or the spontaneous dance moves on the dance floor, my goal is to document these moments in the most authentic way possible.

### **Why do candid photos matter?**

Candid wedding photos aren’t just snapshots; they’re timeless reminders of the emotions, connections, and fleeting moments that make your wedding day unforgettable. They tell the story of your love in a way that photos simply can’t. When you look back at your wedding album years from now, I want you to feel the same rush of emotions and memories that you felt on****[**that magical day in Aruba**](https://www.g10.studio/photography-in-aruba-blog/top-reasons-to-choose-aruba-for-your-perfect-destination-wedding). Not to mention, you’re more likely to be yourself when you don’t know there’s a camera on you, which results in the best photographs. 

## **Final Thoughts**

So, if you’re looking for a luxury destination photographer in Aruba who specializes in capturing candid wedding photos that are filled with love, laughter, and authentic moments, I’d love to speak with you. Let’s create a collection of images that truly reflects the beauty of your special day.
