## **FAQs**

### **Do I need to tell my partner we’re having photos taken?**

Not always. Many couples keep it a surprise. Photographers can stay hidden until the moment happens. If you prefer, you can set up a “couples shoot” so your partner is already camera-ready.

### **What should we wear for a beach proposal shoot?**

Wear clothes that are befitting a beach. Light colored, loose dresses, smart casual will do well with men. Heavy patterns and dark black clothes are also to be avoided because they do not always look good in sunset light.

### **Can photographers handle bad weather during beach proposals?**

Yes. The majority of photographers have contingency plans, such as changing the time or relocating to a covered place, or rescheduling. Gray clouds are always pretty in the photos, and there is no need to worry that the sun is not shining; it can still be enchanting.

### **How far in advance should I book a proposal photographer?**

But preferably, book 4- 6 weeks beforehand. This is because popular photographers in places such as Aruba or Hawaii might require several months to be booked, particularly during the wedding period. The earlier you book, the more assured your date will be.

### **Can family or friends be included in the proposal photos?**

Definitely. In case you want loved ones to be present immediately after the big moment, then inform the photographer. They will even be able to strategize where the guests will be placed because they do not want to ruin the surprise and have all of them in focus.
