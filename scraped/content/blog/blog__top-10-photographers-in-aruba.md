### Pick Your Spot for a Dream Wedding Shoot in Aruba

For truly unforgettable [**locations for a wedding shoot in Aruba**](/blog/how-many-beaches-are-there-in-aruba-for-weddings), consider these spots that blend natural beauty with historical charm.

  * Begin at the **Bushiribana Ruins** , where ancient stone walls meet the island's rugged landscape, providing a dramatic backdrop for your love story.

  * The **Balashi Ruins** are a testament to Aruba's rich history, offering a unique setting for capturing timeless moments.

  * The **Casibari Rocks** present a surreal landscape of towering boulders, perfect for a whimsical photo session.

  * For a romantic waterfront setting, head to the **Hadicurari Marina** , where the sea meets the shore.

  * **Bucuti Beach** offers sheer peace, ideal for intimate moments.

  * Explore **Downtown Oranjestad** for vibrant colors and lively energy.

  * The **California Lighthouse** stands tall, offering sweeping views of the island.

  * Lastly, the **Alto Vista Chapel** and **Wariruri Bay** provide attractive landscapes to make your wedding photos unforgettable.




Each location in Aruba offers a distinct charm, ensuring your wedding shoot is as memorable as the island itself.
