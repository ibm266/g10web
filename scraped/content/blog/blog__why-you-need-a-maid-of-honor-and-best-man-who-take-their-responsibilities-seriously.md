# Why you need a maid of honor and best man who take their responsibilities incredibly seriously

Written By [Jiten Melwani](/blog?author=62ed087ff4f34a1415eacc32)

Your right-hand person plays a pivotal role in ensuring everything runs smoothly, allowing you to relax and fully enjoy your [**Aruban destination wedding**](/blog/how-to-get-the-most-out-of-your-consultation). Here’s why having a maid of honor or best man who’s on task is essential and how they can enhance your wedding experience:

## **Keeping the room tidy for getting-ready shots**

One of the key responsibilities of your right-hand person is to keep the getting-ready room tidy and organized. A clutter-free environment allows your photographer to capture stunning getting-ready shots without any distractions. They make sure that personal belongings are neatly stored, empty glasses are cleared away, the [**photographer gets your wedding**](/blog/preparing-wedding-accessories-for-your-wedding-photographer-to-get-some-detail-shots)details, and any unnecessary items are removed from sight. This attention to detail enables every photo to reflect the elegance and excitement of your wedding morning.

### **Organizing everyone with a personal touch**

Your maid of honor or best man becomes a vital organizer, especially when it comes to coordinating family and friends. Since they know everyone by name, they can efficiently gather groups for family portraits or candid shots. This saves time and means that no important moments are missed. I’ve definitely relied on members of the wedding party to get things done! Whether it’s rounding up the bridal party for a toast or gathering guests for a group photo, their familiarity with your loved ones guarantees a seamless flow of events throughout the day.

### **Being the bride and groom’s go-to for everything**

On your wedding day, you may find yourselves caught up in the whirlwind of emotions and festivities. Your right-hand person acts as your go-to for anything you may need, whether it’s a glass of water, a snack, or a quick touch-up before photos; all you have to do is ask. They should be there to anticipate your needs so that you’re comfortable and relaxed throughout the day. This support allows you to focus on each other and the joyous moments of your celebration without worrying about the details.

### **Creating a stress-free experience**

Having a dedicated right-hand person alleviates stress and allows you to fully immerse yourself in the joy of your wedding day. They’ll handle logistics, manage timelines, and troubleshoot any minor issues that may arise. That way, you and your guests can have an amazing day without worrying about what’s going on behind the scenes. All you have to do is show up, sit back, and relax. Let your friends, wedding photographer, and wedding planner handle the rest! As a thank you afterwards (or before), you can [**organize an activity for your wedding party**](/blog/best-things-to-do-aruba-wedding-party-destination) to do whilst in Aruba.
