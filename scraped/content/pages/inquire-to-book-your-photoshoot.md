#### Before inquiring, please note…

  1. Prices **start** at **$600**.

  2. Please fill in the **“dates in aruba”** question… during busy season last minute inquiries may not get their first choice of date/time! So it helps to be flexible!

  3. We recommend inquiring at least **4 weeks in advance** to secure your spot. 

  4. We **do not take any bookings for the same day** , and rarely for the next day.

  5. Once we receive your inquiry, you will get an email with a booking link where you can see our most **popular packages** and choose your desired date and time to **book with a deposit**.

  6. All photoshoots will take place in Aruba. We have a list of locations & recommendations, however we try to stay away from the Palm Beach area unless you have a large group that cannot travel.

  7. We are more than open to create **custom packages** to suit your needs, and couples can even inquire for **more exotic locations** such as Blackstone Beach, the Colored houses of Oranjestad, Boulder Beach, Quadirikiri Light Caves, and more!




We proudly welcome everyone regardless of religion, gender, race, or sexual orientation.
