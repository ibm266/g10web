## I’m an Aruban, British & Indian life-loving creative. I’ve been a professional photographer for over 7 years, starting in London and now living in Aruba!

The hands-down 2 most important things to me in my life are both **family and memories.**

Family because that’s all you’ll ever have unconditionally. Memories because that’s all you’ll ever have to remember them by.

So… memories of family? Photographs, videos. To me, those are **invaluable**. 

I know how much it means to me, so I know how much it will mean to you. That’s the bottom line to why I do what I do and why I do it so well.

![Photography](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/63b6fd7ba3355c088a58662f/0dcf9f58-215a-4ec2-b131-7bd9a75dd7bb/G1003800.jpg)

I’m an extroverted introvert. Which means your whole family or wedding party gets to know and love my extroverted personality one minute, and the next minute I’m sat 14 days in a row in my office meticulously editing, building my business and learning new skills to offer my best service to you.

I think in order to be a **true** photographer you need to be able to connect with your audience. There are certain qualities one needs to be able to showcase the most authentic, relaxed and beautiful version of one’s subject. When you meet me, you’ll notice this. I like to make it feel like a fun conversation, and I’ll help you pose if I think a certain angle suits you.

From the first email you’ll get to know me - my personality behind the camera makes you wanna laugh and let loose. I’m that guy with no filter! And if it’s your wedding, I love to capture those candid moments, those memories that you would have otherwise said “Oh I wish I had a picture of that moment”.
